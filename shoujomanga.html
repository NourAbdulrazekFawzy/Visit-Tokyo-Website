Shoujo Manga

When talking about Japan, therer is a very popular category that we should be involved with. It is Manga of course. Manga is kind of japanese comics. There is different generes of manga for different people,shounin,shoujo,...and josie,but here we will mention some of our favourite shoujo manga which is suitable for teen girls

6.Koiseyo Mayakashi Tenshi-domo

Otogi Katsura has had a reputation for being a beautiful girl at school since she entered the school. She is also named president of the class committee, but the vice president, Ichi (Ninomae), is also reputed to be a pretty boy. Unlike her public face, Otogi Katsura is an outspoken character and a martial artist in her own right. She is attracted to the perfect Ichi, who is also perfect on the inside, but she wonders if he also has a dark side.

5.Unmei no hito ni Deau Hanashi

Yuuki is a 20-year-old college sophomore who has wished for one thing since moving to Tokyo: to find her soulmate. Growing a bit desperate after a long drought, she goes along with a friend to a club for the first time. Her friend thrives, but it's chaotic and loud… just not Yuuki's scene. Just when Yuuki begins to despair that she'll ever find a real, adult relationship, she meets Iori, a man with bleached hair and a slightly scary demeanor… but first impressions don't always tell the whole story. Could destiny still have more cards to play?

4.Onee chan no Midori kun

The manga centers on Sui, a girl in her third year of junior high. She doesn't really get along with her older sister's boyfriend, Midori-kun, since he always treats her like a kid and makes fun of her. But Midori-kun is suddenly asked to be Sui's home tutor.

3.Hotaru no yomeire

A romance story set in the Meiji era. Born into a prestigious family and blessed with good looks, Satoko is said to have a short life expectancy. But suddenly, her life is targeted by a mysterious killer...?! In order to survive on the spot, Satoko makes a "proposal" for the killer...?! - A young lady and an assassin... A contract marriage love suspense between two people who should never meet!

2.Uruwashi no yoi no tsuki

Yoi is a girl who is called "prince" by those around her and treated like a hero in a girls' manga. Then she meets Ichimura, who is also called "prince." He calls her "beautiful" and saves her from getting tangled up with a drunk at a convenience store, and the two quickly get close! Is this encounter fate? Or is it bad luck?! The story of a man and a woman, both called "princes," begins now!

1.Tsubaki chou: lonely planet

Ohno Fumi is a poor 2nd year high school student. Because of her father's debts, she's kicked out of her own home and has to rely on her own connections to survive. Thus begins her life as a live-in housekeeper for a reclusive writer.
